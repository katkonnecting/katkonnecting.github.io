You're burning in heart
Slow and steady;
Just like at those flowers
In my hands, they were ready.

I can see you
Through the smoke,
You are no longer there
And something in me broke.

I'll wait as long as I can
I don't know why.
You were my moon & my stars
When I dismiss you, I'll look at the sky.

— Aish.kat
